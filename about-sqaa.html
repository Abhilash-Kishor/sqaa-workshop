About SQAA+

School Quality Assessment and Assurance Plus

Introduction

SQAA+ is a collaborative school-improvement effort designed to provide targeted mentoring support to identified schools. Under this initiative, empanelled mentors will provide academic, administrative, and leadership guidance to help schools strengthen teaching-learning processes, governance systems, and performance culture.

Objectives of the Program

  • Build a peer-driven quality improvement ecosystem in CBSE schools
  • Strengthen school leadership, capacity of teaching staff and promote adoption of effective educational practices across schools
  • Help schools set priorities, execute improvement actions, and monitor progress
  • Institutionalize a culture of continuous reflection, collaboration, and innovation

Evolution from SQAAF

The SQAAF framework established structured benchmarks for assessing school quality across CBSE-affiliated institutions. Insights gained from its implementation highlighted the need for sustained support beyond assessment.

SQAA+ addresses this need by strengthening mentoring partnerships, peer learning, and continuous assurance mechanisms to support long-term institutional growth.
