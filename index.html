About Workshop

Launch of the SQAA+ Program



The CBSE SQAA+ National Workshop 2026 serves as the official launch platform for the SQAA+ Pilot Program. This event brings together educational leaders, mentors, and administrators to shape the future of school quality assurance in India. This National Workshop aims to collectively set standards for school improvement in a rapidly changing educational landscape. Recognition of this two-day workshop as an important milestone, offering space to engage with the ideas of school quality through practical scenarios and professional experience, with an emphasis on collective learning.
