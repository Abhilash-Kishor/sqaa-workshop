About Workshop

Launching the SQAA+ Pilot Programme

Workshop Overview

The CBSE SQAA+ National Workshop 2026 serves as the official launch platform for the SQAA+ Pilot Programme. This comprehensive two-day event brings together educational leaders, experienced mentors, and school administrators from across the country to collaborate, learn, and shape the future of school quality assurance in India.

Scheduled for February 2nd & 3rd, 2026, the workshop features expert-led sessions, interactive activities, group discussions, and networking opportunities designed to build capacity and foster strong partnerships for successful SQAA+ implementation.
